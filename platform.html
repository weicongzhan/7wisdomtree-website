Core Module

Flashcard Courses

45+ structured courses with 2000+ core vocabulary words. Each lesson features beautifully illustrated cards with images, Arabic text, Chinese and English translations, native pronunciation, and example sentences.

  • ✓ Browse mode — flip through cards one by one with audio
  • ✓ Quiz mode — match Chinese meanings to Arabic words
  • ✓ Listening mode — hear audio and select the matching image
  • ✓ Progress tracking for each lesson
Multiple Study Modes

Learn, Test & Listen

Each flashcard lesson offers three study modes. Browse all words with pronunciation, test yourself with image-word matching quizzes, or train your listening with audio recognition exercises.

  • ✓ Browse all words — tap to hear native pronunciation
  • ✓ Text quiz — see image & Chinese, choose the Arabic word
  • ✓ Audio quiz — hear pronunciation, pick the right image
Real-Life Scenarios

Reading Passages

257+ sentences across multiple difficulty levels — from beginner to advanced. Practice with real Egyptian dialect conversations set in everyday scenarios like shopping at markets, visiting pharmacies, and more.

  • ✓ Categorized by level: beginner, intermediate, advanced
  • ✓ Scenario-based dialogues with illustrations
  • ✓ Sentence-by-sentence audio playback
  • ✓ Core words highlighted in color, tap any word for details
Immersive Reading

Arabic Picture Books

Beautifully illustrated picture books with full Arabic text, Chinese translation, and English translation. Tap any word to see its definition, pronunciation, example sentence, and image — then add it to your vocabulary notebook with one tap.

  • ✓ Page-by-page reading with auto or manual audio
  • ✓ Trilingual display: Arabic, Chinese, English
  • ✓ Tap any word for detailed popup with image & example
  • ✓ One-tap add to personal vocabulary notebook
Authentic Content

Video Lessons

Watch real Arabic videos from YouTube with synchronized bilingual subtitles. Core vocabulary is highlighted in color — tap any word in the subtitles to learn its pronunciation and meaning instantly.

  • ✓ YouTube video integration with synced subtitles
  • ✓ Arabic-Chinese bilingual subtitle display
  • ✓ Tap subtitle words for instant lookup
  • ✓ Animated stories and real-life content for all ages
Never Forget

Smart Vocabulary Review

All words you encounter across flashcards, reading, picture books, and videos flow into your personal vocabulary notebook. The SM-2 spaced repetition algorithm schedules reviews at optimal intervals so you retain everything you learn.

  • ✓ Dashboard showing total words, due reviews, mastery rate
  • ✓ Review cards with word, image, pronunciation & example
  • ✓ Rate difficulty: Forgot / Hard / OK / Easy
  • ✓ Quick access from reading, picture books, flashcards
