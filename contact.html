FAQ

Common questions about our courses and platform

🆓 What's included for free?

  • After signing up, the first 10 lessons/items in flashcards, readings, picture books, and videos are all free. Experience every feature before deciding to unlock full access.

📱 What devices are supported?

  • We offer a web version (accessible on any computer or mobile browser) and an Android app. All data syncs across devices seamlessly.

🗣️ What languages do you teach?

  • We offer Egyptian Arabic dialect, Modern Standard Arabic (MSA), English, and Chinese (Mandarin). Our Arabic program includes both the practical Egyptian dialect and formal MSA. All languages are available online and in-person.

👶 Can complete beginners learn?

  • Absolutely! Our courses start from the alphabet and pronunciation, progressing step by step. Flashcards include images, bilingual definitions, and audio — perfect for total beginners.
